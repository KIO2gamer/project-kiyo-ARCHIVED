Why Choose Kiyo?

Powerful Moderation

Keep your server safe with advanced moderation tools including ban, kick, mute, warn, and message purging. Automated moderation helps you fight spam and inappropriate content.

Fun & Games

Entertain your community with interactive games, memes, trivia challenges, and word games including unscramble. Keep your server engaged and lively.

Utility Tools

Access translation services, weather forecasts, calculators, search functionality, and more. Practical tools to make server management easier.

Easy Configuration

Customize Kiyo to fit your server's needs through an intuitive dashboard. Configure roles, permissions, welcome messages, and more with just a few clicks.

Welcome System

Create custom welcome messages with automatic role assignment. Make new members feel at home with personalized greetings.

YouTube Integration

Connect YouTube channels to get notifications for new videos. Keep your community updated with your latest content.
